Company Overview and Philosophy

Kilitek Africa Ltd is a leading water treatment solutions provider in Tanzania, specializing in industrial water purification, wastewater treatment, and water quality management. We help businesses reduce water treatment costs by up to 40% with our proven solutions.

Our corporate philosophy is built on a foundation of strong values and standards:

Vision: To be a socially responsible and environmentally friendly provider of water treatment solutions.

Mission: To transform scarce water into safe, clean water for a healthy life, environment, and economy.

Values: Integrity, honesty, ethics, and fairness are at the core of our business practices.

Standards: We emphasize Quality, Usability, Durability, Reliability, Performance, and Service Support in all our offerings.

Services and Business Applications

Kilitek Africa Ltd provides a comprehensive range of services, including:

Water and Wastewater Treatment: We produce high-quality water and offer systems for various industrial, commercial, and domestic applications.

After-Sales Service: Our team of experts provides installation, commissioning, maintenance, and troubleshooting services.

Operation and Maintenance: We offer these services for our own and existing plants to ensure optimal performance.

Water Treatment Chemicals: We utilize advanced technology to ensure water is in optimal condition for various applications.

Our expertise extends across numerous business applications, from treating existing water sources and conducting water audits to serving industries like food processing, dairy, agriculture, and hospitality. We also cater to hospitals, pharmaceuticals (including dialysis), schools, and residential properties.

Extensive Product Range

Kilitek Africa Ltd offers a comprehensive selection of water treatment products, including:

Treatment Plants: Pre-Treatment, Softener, DM (Demineralization), Ultrafiltration (UF), Reverse Osmosis (RO), Effluent Treatment (ETP), and Sewage Treatment (STP) plants.

Filtration Systems: A variety of filter cartridges (String Wound, Spun PP, Pleated PP, Activated Carbon), filter bags, plastic and stainless steel filter housings, and bag filter assemblies.

Components: Multi-port valves, distribution systems, RO membranes, FRP pressure vessels, and Victaulic type couplings.

Instrumentation: Online digital instruments for pH, conductivity, and TDS, as well as pressure switches, flow meters, and pressure gauges.

Media and Chemicals: Water filter media, ion exchange resins, activated carbon, and a range of water treatment chemicals for various applications.

Other Equipment: UV systems, dosing pumps, ozone generators, and water jar and bottle filling machines.
